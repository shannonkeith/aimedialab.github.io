Mission

The AI Multimedia Lab pairs Wayne State's storytelling and communications expertise with AI-accelerated tools and workflows — helping campus partners produce more video, written, web, and PR content without sacrificing quality or brand consistency.

  • One intake point

    Multimedia and communications requests come through a single door instead of four separate queues.

  • Faster turnaround

    AI-assisted production speeds up video, copy, web builds, and press materials.

  • Consistent brand

    The same voice and visual identity across every output, regardless of medium.

  • A proving ground

    New AI tools get tested here before they're considered for adoption university-wide.

What we do

Video Production

Short- and long-form video for recruitment, research storytelling, events, and social — with AI-assisted rough cuts, transcription and captioning, b-roll tagging, and highlight reels.

Writing

News stories, features, scripts, and web copy. AI-assisted drafting, editing, and SEO make it easy to repurpose one interview into an article, social copy, and a script.

Web Development

Landing pages, campaign microsites, and CMS updates — built with AI-assisted prototyping, accessibility checks, and reusable components across university web properties.

Public Relations

Press releases, media pitches, briefing materials, and crisis communications support, backed by AI-assisted media monitoring and press list building.

How it works

  1. 1

    Submit a request

    Tell us your goal, audience, timeline, and available budget or resources.

  2. 2

    Triage

    We route your request to the right discipline, or coordinate across several if it spans video, writing, web, and PR.

  3. 3

    Scope

    The assigned lead confirms deliverables and timeline with you before production starts.

  4. 4

    Produce & review

    We build with AI-assisted tools, then run it through brand, accuracy, and accessibility checks.

  5. 5

    Deliver

    Structured revision rounds, then final assets delivered — or published directly to web and social.
